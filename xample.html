DELICACIES

Experience the rich flavors and unique dishes that showcase Kazakhstan's culinary heritage.

Kazakhstan's national dish made of boiled meat and pasta sheets.

Shashlik

Grilled skewered meat often served with onions and vinegar sauce.

Kurt

Dried, salty cheese balls with a rich, tangy flavor.
